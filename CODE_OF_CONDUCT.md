# Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant.

## Pledge

We commit to a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of identity or background.

## Standards

We expect:
- respectful, constructive communication
- good-faith technical disagreement
- accountability and correction when mistakes happen

We do not tolerate:
- harassment or discrimination
- threats or doxxing
- repeated disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Report issues privately to the maintainers.
Maintainers will investigate and take appropriate action, including warnings or bans.
